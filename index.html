How It Works

Two modes. One seamless experience.

Penpal works in the background by default and steps forward when you need it — no app-switching, no disruptive prompts.

Passive Mode
Always on. Never in the way.
Runs silently while you write — learning, monitoring, and flagging without interrupting your flow.
  • Real-time grammar and clarity monitoring
  • Tone and voice consistency checks
  • Continuous voice profile refinement
  • Surfaces suggestions on your terms
Active Mode
Your co-author. On demand.
When you want Penpal to step in, it rewrites, summarizes, expands, or restructures — always in your voice, not some generic AI's.
  • Rewrite in your voice
  • Summarise or expand sections
  • Restructure for clarity and flow
  • Auto-cite and fact-check on request

Why Penpal

Not another writing tool.
A writing layer.

The Huffman Librarian

Memory that compounds over time.

A self-organizing memory system using embeddings to dynamically prioritize your unique characteristics — tone, vocabulary, stylistic nuances. The more you use it, the better it knows you. That's a moat no competitor can copy.

The Logic Teacher

Arguments that actually hold up.

Constructs dynamic logic pipelines to assess the integrity of your claims and reasoning — not just grammar, but whether your arguments are structurally sound. Critical for research, essays, and high-stakes professional writing.

Cross-Platform Native

Everywhere, not just somewhere.

Unlike tools locked in a single app, Penpal operates at the screen level — simultaneously available in Gmail, Google Docs, Word, Notion, Slack, and any browser. One tool. Every surface you already use.

Privacy First

Your IP stays yours.

Built on a strict no-train architecture. Your unpublished research, sensitive documents, and original writing are never used to fine-tune public models. Isolated processing, full stop.
